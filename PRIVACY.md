## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Data the App Does **Not** Collect

As designed, the Soliplex App does **not**:

- Collect analytics about your usage of the App (no in‑app analytics or tracking SDKs).
- Collect or store your contacts, calendars, photos, microphone, camera, or precise location.
- Track you across apps or websites owned by other companies for advertising or profiling purposes.

If this changes in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and (where required) ask for your consent before collecting additional data.

### 3.2 Data Processed Locally on Your Device

To provide its functionality, the App may process certain information locally on your device, such as:

- Configuration and preference settings you choose within the App.
- Operational data needed to perform the App’s core features (for example, application state, mapping of shortcuts, or similar technical data depending on the final implemented feature set).

This information is typically stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers, unless you explicitly choose to send it to us (for example, as part of a support request).

### 3.3 Data You Provide Directly

You may choose to provide us with personal information when you interact with us outside of the App, such as:

- Contact details (name, email address) when you request support or send feedback.
- Any additional information you include in your communications with us.

We use this information only to respond to your request, provide support, and improve the App.
